Facilities

Classrooms

Our classrooms with benches arranged in rows and a blackboard for instructors provide a traditional yet effective learning environment. Fans, and lights provide a comfortable and conducive learning environment for students. These classrooms are designed to create a sense of community among students, encouraging collaboration and interaction and ample natural lighting, these classrooms offer a conducive space for learning. The arrangement of benches allows students to see and engage with one another, promoting engagement and participation. The blackboard provides a clear surface for instructors to convey important information and facilitate discussions.

Library

Library is back bone of college. It is located on second floor in the college building. The College Provides a very good library system which contains a large number of text books, reference books and subscribes wide range of magazines, periodicals and newspapers.

In addition to this library provide access to thousands of e-resources through Public Access Catalogue (OPAC). The reading room of the college library provides to 100 students at a time. A separate section equipped with required resources has been developed for the students preparing for competitive examinations.

The library is open from 9:00 am till 4:00 pm on all working days. There is a separate reading room for students and faculty members.

Rules of Library

  • Books shall be issued from the issue section, which should be returned on or before the due date mentioned on the date slip
  • In case the book is not returned on the due date, the student shall have to pay a fine at the rate of Rs. 10/- per day.
  • The due date can be extended in specific cases with the approval of librarian.
  • Reference Books will not be issued for home reading or Xeroxing. They should be used only in the library with careful handling. Any damage to the book will entail fine.
  • In case of loss of book, the cost of the same will be recovered. In addition, a fine of 50% of the cost of the book will be levied.

Gymkhana

Gymkhana offers a diverse range of indoor games and activities to cater to different interests and skill levels. From table tennis and carom to chess and board games, our Gymkhana provides a perfect space for students to engage in friendly competitions and showcase their skills. The Gymkhana is spacious and well-maintained, providing a safe and comfortable space for students to relax and unwind. Additionally, the Gymkhana is equipped with modern sports equipment, ensuring that students have access to the best tools and resources to hone their skills and compete at the highest level.

Computer Lab

Our computer lab is the perfect space for Information Technology and Computer Science students to complete their subject practicals and software projects. Equipped with 15 computers, each having an 8GB RAM and Intel i3 processor, our lab provides ample computing power to run the latest versions of software required for these subjects. From programming languages to complex design software, our lab can handle any project or practical task with ease. With access to modern technology, students can develop practical skills that will prepare them for their future careers. Our lab provides a supportive and engaging environment, allowing students to explore and experiment with the latest advancements in computer science. Join us in this state-of-the-art computer lab to gain practical skills and experience that will help you excel in your field of study.

Playground

Our college boasts a well-maintained playground that is ideal for conducting sports events and tournaments. Our playground offers a perfect space for students to showcase their sporting prowess and engage in competitive activities. The playground features well-marked fields for various sports such as football, basketball, and volleyball, and is maintained to the highest standards to ensure the safety and comfort of our students.
