It gives me immense pleasure to welcome you to the website of ICE COLLEGE OF HOTEL MANAGEMENT NAVI MUMBAI. The college constantly thrives to enable students to develop as intellectually alive and socially responsible citizens ever ready for personal as well as professional growth.

As a responsible and caring institution, we pledge to continue to work proactively toward the wellbeing of our students.

Moreover, to provide qualitative education to the students, several curricular, co-curricular activities and Certificate Courses are being offered.

Along with academic programmes we run many short term skills oriented and job oriented programmes too. Our NSS unit is actively functional.

In accordance with the changing economic and educational scenario our college offers different courses. Moreover, to make them competent we have many academic programmes as well as co-curricular activities and I am sure our students will bring laurels to the college by getting ranks in academics at University level as well as winning prizes in extracurricular activities.

About Us

The college is managed by Vidyavardhini Mandal’s, under the dynamic leadership of Shri Sunil Balaram Patil. The trust has to its school, Jr College(State Board). Aim of the trust is to carry out educational, technical and research activities. The degree college has started in the year 2003 - 2004 with B.Com,B.Sc. The college has started degree courses i.e. B Sc (Hospitality studies) for 2024-2025.
